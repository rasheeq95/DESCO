Dear Employees,

At DESCO, we strive to create a positive and productive work environment for all our employees. To ensure that everyone is aware of our company policies and procedures, we have outlined them below.

Code of Conduct:
All employees must conduct themselves professionally and ethically, treating colleagues, customers, and vendors with respect. Confidentiality of sensitive information must be maintained, and all applicable laws and regulations must be followed.

Attendance and Punctuality:
Employees must report to work on time and maintain regular attendance. If absent due to illness or emergency, they must notify their supervisor immediately.

Dress Code:
Employees must dress appropriately for their job function and maintain a clean, neat appearance. Consult with your supervisor if unsure about the appropriate attire.

Workplace Safety:
All employees must maintain a safe and healthy workplace, following safety guidelines, and reporting any hazards or injuries to their supervisor immediately.

Use of Company Property and Resources:
Company property and resources are for work-related purposes only. Personal use must be approved by a supervisor.

Performance Evaluation:
All employees will receive periodic evaluations to discuss job responsibilities, accomplishments, and goals. Employees are encouraged to ask questions and provide feedback.

Grievance Procedure:
If employees have a concern or complaint, they should bring it to their supervisor's attention. If unresolved, escalate it to the HR department.

By following these policies and procedures, we can ensure a positive and productive work environment. Contact your supervisor or HR department with any questions or concerns.

Sincerely,

Manager
DESCO
